# ProjectNYC

Mission:
Turn student signals into operational intelligence.

Problem:
Students see building issues before they become formal work orders.

Solution:
Capture student observations, structure them with AI, connect them to assets and work orders, and recommend actions.

Workflow:

Student Signal
↓
AI Analysis
↓
Evidence
↓
Confidence
↓
Recommended Action
↓
CriticalAsset Update
↓
Verification

Goal:
Make work orders better.

Confidence Labels:
- Verified
- Likely
- Inferred
- Missing
- Needs Inspection
- Conflicting
